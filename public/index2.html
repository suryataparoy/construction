About Us

McHale Utility Services, established in 2006, is a leading multidiscipline construction and environmental company.Operating throughout the UK and Ireland delivered projects within the utilities, housing and energy sectors. We work hard to understand our client's needs and offer innovative, safe and sustainable solutions to the most complex challenges.

    • McHale Utility Services LTD is an established Yorkshire company incorporated in 2006, having worked on projects throughout the UK delivering a cross section of environmental services for a number of clients, within the Energies, Housing, Transportation and Utility sectors.

    The main areas of work which MUS delivers are External Environmental works (which includes new build, infrastructure support, drainage, paving, fencing, new and existing road construction resurfacing, concrete structure and repair etc.) and Specialist Environmental works (which includes Land Remediation, Demolition, Non-licenced Asbestos Contaminated Land Bulk Removal etc.)

    McHale Utilities prides itself on its very high standards of Safety, Quality and Service. Which is delivered to all our clients by ou experienced management team and competent skilled workforce. We have insurances of £10m public and £10m employee liability. MUS is accredited be Chas and Construction Line, Achilles UVDB and ISO 9001 we are also members of the British Safety Council. MUS is currently in the process of attaining ISO 14001, and 18001. At McHale Utilities we believe in straight talking, working with our clients to deliver a good job, safely, for a fair price.
